Gelin

Samet and I have absolutely no hobbies or interests in common at all! I fact, we're pretty much complete opposites! But who needs stuff in common? It's over-rated!

More importantly, we have the same life goals, the same priorities, the same attitudes to people and money and work and the same sense of what's right and wrong in life.
